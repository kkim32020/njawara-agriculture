The Njawara Agriculture Training Site supports local farmers and youth through hands-on training in sustainable agriculture.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve food security and livelihoods by providing practical, community-based agricultural training.

What We Do

We engage in crop production and demonstration plots, vegetable gardening and composting, and provide farmer training and youth engagement.
