The Njawara Agriculture Training Site supports local farmers and youth through hands-on training in sustainable agriculture.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve food security and livelihoods by providing practical, community-based agricultural training.

What We Do

  • Crop production and demonstration plots
  • Vegetable gardening and composting
  • Farmer training and youth engagement

Who We Serve

We work with local farmers, youth, and community members in and around Njawara.

Location

Njawara, The Gambia

This site is managed by the Njawara Agriculture Training Site. Technical support provided by a Peace Corps Volunteer.
